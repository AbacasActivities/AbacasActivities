    Highlights


Abacas Online is a collection of the works by Maura Nicholson, including the website it's self. It's purpose is to be in addition to any social media account I make in the future, which is not truly personalizable.

On the odd standard social madia platform, you get: a choice of a profile picture, maybe a banner, and then a feed of posts that may or may not get shown to people by a mysterious "unbiased algorithm," which on some platforms may oddly not show your posts to your followers anymore.

With my own dedicated website, I get to build the whole user interface. I am front and center until the visitor (you) chooses to click off of the page or closes the tab. The nav-bars? all lead to my content. The pages? My content. If you spend enough time on my site, you may find every nook and cranny of it.
