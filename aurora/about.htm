Why build a whole website?

Abacas Online is a collection of the works of Maura Nicholson, including the website it's self. It's purpose is to justify not making an off-the-shelf online presense, which is not truly personalizable.

On the odd standard social madia platform, you get: a choice of a profile picture, maybe a banner, and then a feed of posts that may or may not get shown to people by a mysterious "unbiased algorithm," which on some platforms may oddly not show your posts to your followers anymore.

With my own dedicated website, I am front and center until the visitor (you) chooses to click off of the page or closes the tab. The nav-bars, all lead to my content. The pages? My content. If you spend enough time on my site, you may find every nook and cranny of it.
