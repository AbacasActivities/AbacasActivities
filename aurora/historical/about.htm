About Abacas Online/Abacas

Why:

         The reason I am developing this website instead of doing what most people do (signing up for an off-the-shelf social media platform: facebook, twitter, etc.) is because I just don't see the point in setting up all those profiles when I can't fully express myself. There are other reasons I don't, like I don't trust those corperations nor do I enjoy staring at their User Interface design choices.

Who:

        Who am I? My real first name is Maura, but online I will go on as Abacas. No, I can't use one. I'm an overall creative person, who wants to express themselves to the highest of my ability, which in this case has fueled the creation of this website.

What:

        This website has the purpose of being a collection of my artistic works, and a home for myself on the web. I think it would be most accurate to call it a box of my digital stuff, or shortcuts to it at least.
