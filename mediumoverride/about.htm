About Medium Override

About the Project

Medium Override is a big project by Maura Nicholson, the creator of AbacasOnline. It is a manga in the Magical Girl genre with a trans main protagonist, and a world that resides in a simulation. Maura is using this whole project for all three of her defenses, (The big event/project framework at her school) starting with writing, moving to the development of the comic, then finishing with any possible distrobution efforts.

Synopsis

Meet Davey Higgons and follow her through her story, where she blossoms into a beautiful Daisy. We follow her through her missions, where with the help of Janet Jones, she overrides her body to gain access to supernatural powers and a cool uniform. They battle and cure the people who corrupt, or in simple terms, become monsters. Is that all there is to it? Of course not! An evil Corperation lurks in the shadows, exploiting weaknesses in the fabric of reality to make more people corrupt!
