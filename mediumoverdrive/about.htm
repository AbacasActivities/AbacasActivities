To sum it up:

Davey Higgons is an interesting soul, quite literally. When Janet, her mother's tinkerer friend makes technology to bypass the body and give the user's essence form, Davey and Janet are surprised to find that Davey might actually be a girl. Through the first few chapters Davey figures out whether or not this is true, and blooms once she finds out. After that, who knows what problems will occur?
